ABOUT US

Mankind Medicare is a Licensed Pharmacy retail outlet in Northern India. The Pharmacy was incorporated on March 2020. It is operating under a valid Drug Licence issued by the Government of India under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940. We are focused on better patient services with the right and professional approach.
